Spaghetti Recipe

This is a simple recipe for spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Angel Hair Pasta
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1 lbs Ground Beef
  • Personal choice of seasoning

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic and add it to a pan on medium heat
  2. Add the ground beef and allow the beef to brown
  3. Add the sauce to the beef and mix and turn on low heat
  5. In a large pot boil water
  6. Place desired amount of angel hair in rolling boil
  7. Allow pasta to boil for 4 minutes stirring occasionally
  8. Plate the pasta and top with the meat sauce
