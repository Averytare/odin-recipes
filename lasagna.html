Lasagna

Tender sheets of pasta, a creamy ricotta filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce.

This recipe will teach you how to make this popular dish of Italian origin.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Beef and Sausage
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Oinion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parsley
  • Parmesaen

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer & bake
